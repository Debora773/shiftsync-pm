## Store Owner interview

**Goal:** Understand business priorities, constraints, and definition of success.

Questions:
1. How does the current scheduling process affect the business? What does it cost you (time, errors, staff frustration)?
2. What would a better process look like from your perspective?
3. What would make you confident this tool is working?
4. What would make you stop using it?

## Store Manager interview

**Goal:** Understand the real scheduling workflow in detail — the steps, the pain, the edge cases.

Questions:
1. Walk me through how you create a schedule today, step by step.
2. What takes the most time? What do you do manually that you wish was automatic?
3. What are the most common problems that happen after the schedule is published?
4. What information do you need to make a good schedule that you don't always have?
5. If this tool did one thing perfectly, what would that be?
6. [Add problem-specific questions here]

## Staff interview

**Goal:** Understand how the schedule affects daily life and what fairness means to them.

Questions:
1. How do you find out about your schedule? How far in advance?
2. What's the most frustrating thing about how scheduling works right now?
3. Have you ever felt a schedule was unfair? What happened?
4. What would make you feel like the schedule process respects your time?
5. [Add problem-specific questions here]
